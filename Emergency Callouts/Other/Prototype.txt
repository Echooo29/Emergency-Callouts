[Burglary]

Scenarios:
- Attack
- Pursuit
- Surrender

Inspect Property:
1: The suspect's spot gets a flashing blip and tells you to inspect
2: Go to the spot where the suspect was breaking in
3: You can press a key to inspect the property
4: You hold a clipboard and a pencil and look at the property
5: After 15 seconds, you get a 50% chance of damage.
6: After inspection, you talk to the suspect about his reasoning and player mentions the possible damages
7: After dialogue ends, the callout ends with the advanced ending feature.
